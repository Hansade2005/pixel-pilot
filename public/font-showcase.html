Why OpenDyslexic Matters

OpenDyslexic is a typeface specifically designed for people with dyslexia. It was created by Abelardo Gonzalez to help improve readability for dyslexic readers.

The font uses unique letter shapes that are less likely to be confused with each other, making reading more accessible and less frustrating.
